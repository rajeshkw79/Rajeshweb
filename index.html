// The product

Not a chatbot.
An agent that runs your tasks.

You teach it once — by walking through the task or describing it in plain language. It handles it from then on, on a schedule, in your format.

// How it works

Three steps.
Then it just runs.

Most AI requires you to re-explain every time. We built something different.

Step 01

Teach it once

Walk through the task as you normally would, or describe it in plain language. The agent learns your format, your voice, your definitions — and remembers permanently.

Step 02

Set the schedule

Tell it when to run — every Monday at 8am, every Friday EOD, the 1st of every month. It runs automatically. No prompts. No reminders. It just happens.

Step 03

Review the output

Your task is ready. Review, approve, send. Every run is logged — full audit trail. Nothing goes out without your sign-off.

// FAQ

Questions you'll have
before you sign up.

Honest answers. No marketing speak.

Most tasks take 10–20 minutes to set up for the first time. You walk through the task once — either by doing it while the agent watches, or by describing it in plain language. After that, it runs automatically. No coding. No configuration files. If your task is complex (e.g. pulling from 5 sources and formatting to a specific template), expect 30 minutes and one round of refinement.
At launch: Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Docs, Notion, Slack, and file uploads (CSV, XLSX, PDF, DOCX). CRM integrations (for sales follow-ups) and calendar access (for meeting summaries) are coming in the 4 weeks following launch. Enterprise plan includes custom integrations on request.
Your data is processed to run your tasks and is never used to train our models. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not store the content of your documents beyond what's needed to run the task and maintain the audit log. Enterprise plan includes on-premises deployment for teams that can't use cloud infrastructure.
Nothing goes out without your approval. Every task run produces a draft that you review, edit, and approve before it's sent or filed. The agent also keeps a full log of every run — what input it used, what it produced, and what you approved. If a run is wrong, you can correct it and the agent learns from the correction for next time.
Yes — any time, from the web app or the mobile app. You can pause a task for a specific week (e.g. holiday), change the schedule, or turn it off entirely. You can also trigger a task manually at any time outside its schedule, which uses one of your monthly task runs.
